new media ▪ audiovisual communication ▪ multimedia

Marcelo is a media artist creating audiovisual communication and multimedia. An avid wanderer, he listens to the sounds of spaces and researches its uses in field recordings, soundscapes, audio spatialization, and sound art. Passionate about digital art and new media, he's been designing ecosystemic experiences as collaborative spaces of self-reflection through interaction.

Artwork has been exhibited in media festivals such as Porto Post Doc, Semibreve, and Espaço MIRA. A part of COLECTIVO 「大音量コンピューター」, a multimedia collective.

MSc in Multimedia by the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto + BA in Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia by Lusófona University of Porto.
