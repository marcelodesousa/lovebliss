about me

marcelo is a multimedia designer creating audiovisual communication, interaction design, digital art and new media.

An avid wanderer, he listens to the sounds of spaces and researches its uses in field recordings, soundscapes, audio spatialization, and sound art. Passionate about ecosystemic art, he's been designing collaborative spaces and fostering self-reflection through interactive artwork.

Artwork exhibited in Porto Post Doc, Semibreve, and Espaço MIRA. A part of COLECTIVO 「大音量コンピューター」, a multimedia collective.

MSc in Multimedia by the University of Porto + BA in Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia
