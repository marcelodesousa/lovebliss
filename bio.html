bio

Marcelo is a media artist focusing on field recordings, soundscape studies, audio spatialization and sound art.

He currently balances his sonic aspirations with freelancing activities in producing audiovisual content.

He holds a MSc in Multimedia by the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto and a BA in Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia by Universidade Lusófona do Porto.

A part of COLECTIVO「大音量コンピューター」(loud volume computer), a multidisciplinary collective focused on the development of new media art, such as a vague sense of presence, an audio installation part of Festival Semibreve 2018, that explores the symbiosis between individual sounds and the encompassing aural field.
